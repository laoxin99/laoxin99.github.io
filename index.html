CognitiveBridge-v0 Research Roadmap

Human Reaction Mechanism

A theoretical framework for describing shared reaction-level patterns behind behavior, cognition, emotion, and intelligent action.

Status: book project and public preview planning.

ZhiNao

A mechanism-based intelligence research line exploring internal reaction, adjudication, persistence, boundary control, and long-range behavior guidance.

Status: internal evolution line, selective public exhibition.

CognitiveBridge-v0

A minimal offline demo showing how an external thought stream can be constrained into low-permission candidates instead of directly controlling actions.

Status: private pre-public package, GitHub Actions passing.
